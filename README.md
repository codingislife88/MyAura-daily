# ✨ MyAura Daily

A simple, mobile-friendly web app to track your daily mood and wellness habits.

## Features

✨ **Mood Tracking** - Rate your mood from 1-10 with fun emojis
😰 **Stress Level** - Track your daily stress (1-10)
😴 **Sleep** - Log hours of sleep
🏃 **Exercise** - Record minutes of exercise
📱 **Screen Time** - Track hours spent on screens
🌞 **Outside Time** - Log minutes spent outside
💭 **Daily Notes** - Write down your thoughts and feelings
📊 **Dashboard** - View all past entries
📈 **Quick Stats** - See average mood, sleep, and more
💾 **Local Storage** - All data saved on your device

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Fill out today's entry with your mood, habits, and notes
3. Click **"Save Today's Entry"** to store your data
4. View past entries in the dashboard section
5. Filter entries by timeframe (All, Last 7 Days, Last 30 Days)
6. Check your stats to see trends
